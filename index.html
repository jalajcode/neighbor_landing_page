Why Neighbor?

Neighbor is the way to find immediate solutions for the problems that need to be solved instantly.

  • Best way to find help.

    Have you ever been in a situation where you want help but there was no one in your house? What did you do? You must have have postponed it. Use this app and have your neighbor helping you.

  • Offer help.

    Do you have some skills, which most people doesn't have? Use this app, and take help from your neighbors to utilize these skills and help them at the same time.

  • Get to know your neighbor.

    How often do you talk to your neighbors? Use this app and improve your bond with the people around you.

What inspired us?

The problem that we are facing today is that people have a lack of communication with our neighbors, now this may be due to the fact that we come from different backgrounds which has created fear amongst ourselves and makes us judge that person when we don’t even know who they are, also it has made us very cautious of exchanging ideas and offering help to them.

Neighbor is designed to provide you with people around that lives in your community that has certain skills to help you with problems that you face every day. Let’s face it why hire a person from that is located miles away from and you, when you can find someone that that lives right next to you, plus during the COVID time we realize how important it is to support your local community while spreading less of the virus.

The idea is to build a community around locals to be more efficient and to save time and effort and create a stronger bond with your neighbors.
